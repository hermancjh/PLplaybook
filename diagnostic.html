How does your school compare to Lion Secondary?

Four short questions. The output is a written comparison, not a score — its job is to help you decide what to read closely next, not to rate your school.

1. How would you describe your school's current PLC culture?
2. What's your biggest current tension?
3. How experienced is whoever is leading the PL agenda?
4. Is your School Management Team broadly supportive, lukewarm, or skeptical of change right now?
